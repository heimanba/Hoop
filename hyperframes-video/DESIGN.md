# DESIGN.md

## Style Prompt

Create a warm, product-focused Hoop capability film that explains the app as an AI coach built around a single sports video. The piece should feel clear, trustworthy, and gently editorial rather than emotional-for-emotion's-sake. Use a light canvas, real iOS captures, and brand-led orange emphasis to make the product feel grounded and immediately understandable. The tone is not a startup launch trailer and not a family documentary; it sits in the middle: precise enough to explain capability, warm enough to feel human.

## Colors

- `#F5F1EA` canvas: warm off-white base
- `#F06633` brand accent: Hoop orange for primary emphasis
- `#139B95` AI accent: teal for AI coach and guidance states
- `#3F6FCD` game accent: restrained blue for match-related signals
- `#171310` primary text: warm charcoal for high-contrast type

## Typography

- `"Iowan Old Style", "Songti SC", serif` for headlines and key statements
- `"PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", sans-serif` for body, labels, and UI support

## Motion Rules

- Use soft blur crossfades as the primary transition language
- Let major copy arrive with confidence, not hype: layered entrances over 0.45s to 0.9s
- Hold product UI long enough to read the interface and supporting text in one pass
- Use motion to move attention between question, UI, and answer rather than to create spectacle

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon AI visuals, chrome sci-fi treatments, or dark tech gradients
- Do not cut like a sports highlight reel or use impact trailers as reference
- Do not overfill scenes with too many callouts or too much small text
- Do not frame the product as replacing coaches, parents, or practice itself
- Do not introduce extra brand colors beyond the orange-led neutral system with small teal/blue accents
